Portuguese Steak

You’ll find this classic steak dish on just about every Portuguese restaurant’s menu. What makes this dish so flavorful is the combination of red wine, garlic and olive oil which creates a rich sauce that is poured over the steak and egg. This recipe is from my Taste Portugal cook book! I had the book marks printed for my first book signing! The Galo de Barcelos brings good luck!

  • Ingredients for 4 servings

    • 4 steaks (Beef), about 180 g / 6 oz. each
    • 150 g (5 oz.) butter
    • 2 tbsp. oil
    • 4 cloves of garlic, peeled
    • 50 ml (3 tbsp.) dry red wine
    • 50 ml (3 tbsp.) vinegar
    • 2 bay leaves
    • Salt and pepper
    • 1 kg (2 1/4 lb.) potatoes
    • 4 slices of bacon
    • Flat leaf parsley or cilantro

  • Preparation

    1. season the beef with salt and pepper; let rest 10 minutes;
    2. meanwhile, fry the potato slices in butter; season with salt and pepper and set aside;
    3. heat the butter and oil in a skillet; sauté the garlic for a few seconds; add the bay leaf and cilantro;
    4. add the meat and sear on both sides over high heat;
    5. transfer to a ceramic dish; cover with the bay leaf and slices of bacon;
    6. add the wine and vinegar; place into a hot oven and cook for 30-45 minutes;
    7. remove the bay leaf;
    8. correct the seasoning.

    Presentation

    1. place the potatoes all around the steak; garnish with a little parsley or cilantro;
    2. serve immediately.
